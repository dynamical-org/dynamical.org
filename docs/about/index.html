dynamical.org

Fast, Easy, Open Weather Data for Everyone

dynamical.org is a not-for-profit organization with a mission to advance humanity‚Äôs ability to access, understand, and act on accurate weather and climate data.

The organization is designed for long-term operations and stewardship using what we call "Public Benefit Corporation, Hardcore Mode." In this model we explicitly:

  1. prohibit sale of the organization;
  2. require (hypothetical) profit be reinvested in the organization's purpose; and
  3. set organizational priorities led by active members and users (for example, our committees).

The Problem: Weather Data Today

Weather and climate data is critical but often difficult to access, fragmented, and slow to utilize.

The Solution: Zarr and a Community Infrastructure

Zarr is a modern, cloud-native format that makes large multidimensional datasets easy to read and fast to access.

dynamical.org creates a public, live-updating catalog of Zarr-formatted weather datasets that are:

  • Easy: Load with one line of code.
  • Fast: Read performance optimized for real-time analysis and fast experimentation.
  • Open: No lock-in, free access, open source.
  • Consistent: Standardized structure across all datasets.

We are certainly not the first to make public zarrs of weather data. What‚Äôs new is a long-term commitment to maintaining the open-source code and open data repositories we produce and an organization purpose-built to do just that.

Why? We want the data and code produced to be stewarded by an organization that will be here for the long-term. None of us want to build on a foundation that shifts or crumbles in a few years. The result is a stewardship organization designed to operate independently and perpetually.
