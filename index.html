dynamical

advancing humanity's ability to access, understand, and act on past, present and future weather

Weather touches everything But step changes in forecast accuracy, data access, and usability have been elusive.

We are a small, cooperatively governed team, commited to staying independent.

We're just getting started and plan to publish 1-2 emails per month. Want to tag along?

Our plan:

  1. Create free and open zarr format real-time and archive access to the best public-domain data
  2. Operationalize nascent AI forecast models and publish their forecasts
  3. Launch an ensembler to surface the best guidance from all available forecasts
  4. Launch Dynamical weather models

It's crucial we build an enduring business. To financially support our open data, public research, and ongoing R&D, we will:

  • Pursue grant funding from organizations interested in advancing access to and quality of weather forecasts
  • Deploy the weather models we operationalize for enterprise and government customers
  • Build and sell subscription access to vertical-specific decision-support applications
  • Sliding-scale financial support from for-profit companies that get value from our data and services
