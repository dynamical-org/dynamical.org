A lab advancing how we access, understand, and act on data about our world.

dynamical.org is a public catalog of cloud-optimized weather and climate data. We republish forecasts and analyses from models like NOAA GFS, GEFS, HRRR and MRMS, ECMWF IFS and AIFS, and DWD ICON as free, analysis-ready Zarr archives you can open directly with Xarray — no GRIB wrangling, no bulk downloads.
