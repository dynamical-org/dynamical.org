Build critical training and operational infrastructure on dynamical.org

dynamical.org publishes an analysis-ready, multi-model Icechunk Zarr weather catalog. Our commercial SLA supports the operation and development of the dynamical.org catalog and services, and adds a dedicated support relationship, operational guarantees, and performance remedies.

Free, public access stays free

The public dynamical.org catalog is free and open, and that is not going away — free access is on a best-effort basis with no SLA. The commercial SLA exists to sustain and improve the reliability of that free access, not to paywall it.

What the commercial SLA adds

  • 99.5% uptime on the data-serving path (STAC + hot-storage reads), measured monthly from multiple regions.
  • A committed per-data-product latency window, measured from source release — with sub-10-second update latency for virtualized data products and rapid timeliness for rechunked data products. Fully auditable.
  • Data-pipeline webhooks the moment a model run starts, makes progress, or completes. Optionally attach a function: small Python that runs in a sandbox the instant the event fires, with the dataset preloaded as an xarray.Dataset, returning a summary, a threshold alert, or a rendered plot on the payload — no compute of your own to run.

Built to last

The commercial SLA funds the sustainable operation and growth of dynamical.org, a public-benefit not-for-profit. The data products are hosted on a combination of open-data programs and commercial storage contracts, with redundancy built in to mitigate potential changes in open-storage programs.

Supporting dynamical.org leads to the deployment of new data products, and commercial contract holders help drive our roadmap. Every commercial contract directly improves the reliability and longevity of the free, public access that the broader community depends on.
