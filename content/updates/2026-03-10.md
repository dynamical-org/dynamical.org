## GFS analysis is live

Our old `noaa-gfs-analysis-hourly` was our "Hello World" — 4 variables, static, Zarr v2.

It has been given a lovely retirement party and replaced with the new [GFS analysis](https://dynamical.org/catalog/noaa-gfs-analysis/).

- **Live!** (not static)
- **21 variables** (up from 4)
- Available in **Zarr v3 and Icechunk**

## HRRR analysis back to 2014

Four more sweet years. High Resolution Rapid Refresh has been retroactively retained through repeated re-retrieval - and now, re-released for a more uh... robust retrospective. It previously started in 2018 and now reaches back to [2014](https://dynamical.org/catalog/noaa-hrrr-analysis/). The early years (HRRR v1/v2 era) have more missing source files — NaN values where data are unavailable — but that's the archaeology tax.

Icechunk note: we've updated to v0.2.0. We'll be stopping updates to v0.1.0 on <DATE>, so please switch over.

## Experimental ASOS GeoParquet

I tried (and tried [and tried]) to find a good [citation needed] open source of obs once ISD was decomissioned and GHCNh made me want to cry enough tears to register on the precip gauge. Alas. 

Those tireless little weather stations at airports across the US — are available as a GeoParquet dataset. 1940ish - present. This is our first non-model dataset and it's 100% experimental, but it updates every hour and it's what the [scorecard](https://dynamical.org/scorecard) is built on.

## DWD ICON-EU grib archive

As a first step towards cloud-optimized ICON-EU Zarrs, we've started archiving DWD's gribs on [Source Coop](https://source.coop/dynamical/dwd-icon-grib) — DWD only maintains a short rolling window on their servers, so someone had to hoard them. Data hoarding is our North Star! 

Big thanks to [Jack Kelly](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jackkelly0/) of [Open Climate Fix](https://openclimatefix.org/) for leading this effort and to the [Patrick J McGovern Foundation](https://www.mcgovern.org/) for supporting the work.
